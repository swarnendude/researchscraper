These specific platforms, like those before them, will eventually wane. The rise of sophisticated AI agents promises to accelerate this transition.

In a world rapidly taking shape, each of us could have our own AI agents acting as both curators and filters for our informational lives. You can imagine your AI agent sorting through the incessant noise, becoming a source for insights and recommendations tailored precisely to you. It bypasses the need to follow hundreds or thousands of disparate human voices; instead, it synthesizes vast streams of information, presenting options and perspectives aligned with your interests, values, and cognitive style. Influence itself shifts: from a broadcast model (one-to-many) dominating the social media era, towards a model of personal synthesis (AI-to-one).

For now, let‚Äôs park the issues pertaining to an ‚ÄúAI-filtered world.‚Äù But I assure you, there are several. Not least of which include biased world views shaped by the AI models, echo chambers, and more. Stay tuned for part 2.

In an AI-mediated world, the signal from a trusted human potentially becomes more valuable as it passes through more rigorous filters. Crucially,

who

Influence becomes less about mass appeal or follower counts cultivated through algorithmic luck, and more on demonstrable alignment with the specific values, interests, and trust criteria defined by the individual user and their agent. The paradigm shifts away from sheer visibility towards perceived, verifiable value.

This deeper seeking is already evident in the growing preference for thoughtful niche creators over generic noise. The rise of Substack and newsletters, the rapid growth of podcasts‚Äîwe are taking more control over where we place our attention. A trend AI agents could accelerate by allowing users to more directly guide their agents towards sources offering the substance and resonance they seek ‚Äì driven by admiration, the desire to learn, or shared interests.

The incentive structure for creators and contributors, however, doesn't vanish; it shifts.
